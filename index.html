WELCOME

Hello, and welcome to thekissicks.com. We're very excited to see all our invitees on the 6th of August, and we've built this little website to help make sure that things run as smoothly as possible for you all.

There's lots of useful information on here, from details about the wedding day itself and our gift list, to recommended transport routes and accomodation. Finally, if you're planning on staying for a few extra days, we've even compiled a quick reference of the best websites for finding out about local activies and places to see.

In the mean time, feel free to drop us an email at info@thekissicks.com.
