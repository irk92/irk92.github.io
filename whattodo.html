Staying a while?

We both feel greatly blessed to be able to host our wedding in Bangor, a small city, nested between the mountains and the sea. While we're ecstatic to welcome many of our guests to Bangor just for the day, we know that there will be those of you who'd like to see more of North Wales, take in the seaside air, or simply stick around a little longer to take a late train or ferry home.

To that end, we've put together a quick guide using some information and photos from Bangor University's Management Centre, to give you a flavour of some nearby historical sites. For any extra information, we recommend Vist Wales, Vist Wales and Vist Wales,
